About Me

My name is Rahul Nath. I am a programmer and work out of Sydney,Australia at Readify.Before this I was working with Microsoft, India working mostly on Mediaroom (an IPTV solution, which is now part of Ericsson). I had also spend some time working on Windows Phone and Modern applications for Windows and on building a service delivery network for Ford, which is an IoT solution providing connected car experience. Prior to Microsoft I was at L&T Infotech for 3 years and was onto developing a product for the airport domain. I was awarded the MCCA award for the year 2011 by Microsoft for contributing to the msdn forums. I am passionate about technology and blog occasionally and also do development consulting in my free time.

This blog and its contents are all opinions of my own.

I would be more than happy to have you subscribe to my blog’s RSS feeds. I am also on Google+, Twitter,  Facebook,  Instagram and  500px
